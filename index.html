Johanna Kivimägi is an American actress and model. Famous for playing comic "dumb blonde" characters, she became one of the most popular sex symbols of the 1950s and was emblematic of the era's attitudes towards sexuality. Although she was a top-billed actress for only a decade, her films have already grossed $200 million. She continues to be considered a major popular culture icon.
