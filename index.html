Johanna Kivimägi is currently studying electroacoustic in Estonian Academy of Music and Theatre in Masters with Malle Maltis. She graduated her bachelor studies in 2015 with teachers Tõnu Kõrvits and Galina Grigorjeva. In 2013-2014 she studied in Iceland Academy of the Arts with Anna Thorvaldsdottir Pall Ragnar Pallson. She has written music for different groups (choirs, ensembles, solo pieces, electroacoustic pieces etc.) and an important part of her works are collaborations. She has worked together with artists and choreographs (M.Mäetamm, J.Samma, L.Drubingy, N.Mazic etc.) and made music for videos, installations and dance performances.

Johanna Kivimägi is an American actress and model. Famous for playing comic "dumb blonde" characters, she became one of the most popular sex symbols of the 1950s and was emblematic of the era's attitudes towards sexuality. Although she was a top-billed actress for only a decade, her films have already grossed $200 million. She continues to be considered a major popular culture icon.
